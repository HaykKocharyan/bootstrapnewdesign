    Card strategy with a changing field

    Do you like challenging card battles? Faeria opens up new possibilities for tactical battles - in the course of the next hot battle, you can change the battlefield at your discretion. It is convenient to replenish the collection of cards with fast micropayments with a practical option of infinity - you do not need to purchase add-ons for the sake of one card, you choose only what you need.

    The single player mode is designed for more than 100 hours of play - almost limitless possibilities for cunning strategic maneuvers. Also, you will have joint tasks and puzzles. For the strong in spirit, a special Pandora mode has been developed, where in a tournament format you can test the power and strength of your deck. The game has an extremely close-knit community - a forum and a subreddit, Faeria's The Hub, and its own Discord channel. Tournaments with prizes in the form of useful bonuses and valuable game items are regularly held among the players.

    Visually, Faeria is made in a fabulous style. These are bright colors, cute game animation, colorful designs of the cards themselves and playing fields. Start small, progressing gradually from a beginner to an advanced cardmaster that Faeria fans around the world want to compete with!
